# OSWars Academy

**OSWars** is a long-running Scratch community competition where participants design and build their own concept operating systems, pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the platform. Each iteration brings new challengers, new ideas, and a higher bar for creativity and technical craft. **OSWars Academy** is our educational initiative dedicated to helping Scratch creators level up their skills. Through in-depth tutorials and guides, Academy covers advanced techniques that go far beyond the basics and will help creating complex projects (including operation systems) of any kind.

Whether you're a competition veteran or just getting started, OSWars Academy is built to help you grow.
